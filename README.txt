Open the flocking behavior scene to begin

You can use WASD keys to control the leader, which is the white cube in the scene.

You can use arrow keys to control the camera.

If you click the LazyFlight button, the flock will fly to the generated random point in the scene,
which is represented by a red sphere, when it reached the destination, the sphere will be destroyed,
then a new destination will be generated.

If you click the Follow Leader button, the flock will follow the leader, which is the white cube in the scene, 
you can control the movement of leader by pressing WASD, the flock will move following the leader.
